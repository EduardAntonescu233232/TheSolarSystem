The Solar System

The Solar System is a vast and intricate system of celestial bodies, dominated by the Sun, a star that contains more than 99.8% of the Solar System's total mass. It consists of eight planets, which are, in order of their distance from the Sun: Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. These planets orbit the Sun due to its gravitational pull.
